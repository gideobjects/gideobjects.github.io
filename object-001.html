GIDE Mount

Technical Notes

The arm geometry was derived from a simple cantilever model with a 4 kg point load at the tip. Wall thickness varies along the arm — thicker at the root, tapering toward the tip — producing the visual taper while maintaining constant stress distribution. There is no unnecessary material.

The mounting plate uses a single M5 bolt in a vertical slot, allowing fine height adjustment on installation. A friction-fit cover cap conceals the bolt head — retained by interference fit only. No adhesive, no tools required to remove.

Material and process:

  • ASA chosen over ABS for UV stability — no topcoat required
  • 0.2 mm layer height, 4 perimeters, 40% gyroid infill
  • Matte black filament — surface texture is inherent, not applied
  • Mounting hardware: A2 stainless steel M5 hex socket
  • Wall plate: 3 mm mild steel, powder-coated black
  • Tested load: 6 kg static, 1000-cycle dynamic at 2 kg
