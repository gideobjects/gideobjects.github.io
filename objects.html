Object 001 // Wall Anchor

GIDE Mount

A wall-mounted display hook engineered for jerseys and everyday garments. Geometry derived from strict structural load requirements. Finish chosen for how it tactilely ages over time, not how it photographs.

  • Load Optimization: Generous surface contact base to minimize drywall shear stress.
  • Material Truth: Zero artificial surface coatings. Pure unvarnished grain and raw material properties.
  • Complete Documentation: Package contains structural stress breakdown and install templates.
