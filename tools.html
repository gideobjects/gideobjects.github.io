Tool 02 // Chrome Extension

IntentLink

Bypass ad-heavy results, SEO spam, and AI-generated clickbait. IntentLink instantly detects your search intent and routes you directly to authoritative platforms.

  • Direct Routing: Intelligent mapping for coding, medical, legal, and financial queries.
  • Noise Filtering: Drops e-commerce farms (AliExpress/Temu) in favor of Reddit and Wirecutter.
  • Privacy First: Zero configuration, lightweight background architecture, no registration.
